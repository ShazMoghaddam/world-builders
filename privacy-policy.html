Privacy Policy — World Builders

Last updated: 25 March 2026

Overview

World Builders ("the App") is a children's educational app developed by Shaz Moghaddam. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information. The short version: we collect nothing, store nothing on servers, and share nothing with anyone.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data is stored locally on your device only.

The only information stored locally includes:

  • A chosen display name (e.g. "Builder" or a child's first name)
  • A chosen avatar emoji
  • Game progress (bricks earned, zones unlocked, question history)
  • Bingo board state and daily streak count

This data never leaves the device. It is stored using Android's SharedPreferences system and can be deleted at any time by resetting progress within the app or uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

World Builders is designed for children aged 5–10. We are committed to protecting children's privacy online. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and UK GDPR:

  • We do not collect any personal information from children
  • We do not require account registration or login
  • We do not display advertising of any kind
  • We do not include third-party analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not use the internet connection for any purpose

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party libraries, all of which operate entirely on-device:

  • Flutter & Dart SDK (Google) — app framework
  • google_fonts — downloads fonts on first use, then caches locally
  • audioplayers — plays local audio files
  • provider — state management (no network calls)
  • shared_preferences — local device storage only

None of these libraries transmit personal data to external servers.

Permissions

The App requests no special Android permissions. It does not access the camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other device feature beyond local storage.

Data Retention and Deletion

All app data is stored only on the user's device. You can delete all data by:

  • Using the "Reset all progress" button in the app's Profile screen
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at the URL listed on the Google Play Store listing.
